About Us

We are a leading design and build firm which is based in Delhi with more than 16+ years of specialized experience. Our main aim is to provide our clients with customized and personalized designs and build a solution that goes beyond their imagination. We have completed more than 450 projects in residential, hospitality, retail, and corporate sector across more than 100 cities all over India. We value time and quality as essential in delivering our projects, for which we have extensive in-house capabilities with trained, dedicated, and motivated teams. We work passionately with our clients to achieve lavish results which they cherish for a lifetime. Creativity, integrity, quality, and importance of time are the values that guide us every day. These are the main part of who we are and what we stand for. Here at Design Symposium, we cater to the needs and requirements of the clients, thus giving them a comfortable space and dream that they desire. We are a trusted interior and architectural designing company with key specialization in execution. We take pride in ourselves for our professional design knowledge and expertise in execution.
